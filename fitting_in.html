Fitting in transfer, person of color, transition As a transfer student, I was so excited about coming to UC Davis. But sometimes I was also worried I might be different from other students, particularly as a person of color. And when I got to campus, sometimes it felt like everyone else was right at home. Eventually, I figured out that almost everyone comes to college unsure whether they will fit in or not. Once I realized that, I was able to push myself to reach out and make friends and eventually found a good group of friends. It’s ironic—everyone comes to college and feels they are different from everyone else when, really, in at least some ways, we are all pretty similar.
